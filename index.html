About the Project

AI-Driven Moringa Health Diagnostics

This project applies state-of-the-art deep learning to automatically classify Moringa leaf diseases from photographs, enabling fast and accurate field diagnostics.

Moringa oleifera — known as the "miracle tree" — is a vital crop in tropical and subtropical regions. Disease outbreaks can devastate yields. Our system provides instant, trustworthy diagnosis directly from leaf images, supporting farmers, agronomists, and researchers.

Disease Reference

Disease Classification Classes

Our system identifies four distinct leaf conditions in Moringa plants — three pathological and one healthy reference state.

🔴
Bacterial Leaf Spot
Caused by bacterial pathogens such as Pseudomonas spp. Characterized by water-soaked, dark brown angular lesions surrounded by yellow halos on leaf surfaces. Spreads rapidly in humid conditions and can cause severe defoliation.
Pathological
🟡
Cercospora Leaf Spot
A fungal disease caused by Cercospora moringicola. Presents as circular to irregular grayish-white spots with dark purple-brown borders. Often appears during the wet season and affects leaf quality and yield.
Pathological
🌿
Healthy Leaf
A normal, unaffected Moringa leaf showing uniform green coloration, intact texture, and no visible lesions or discoloration. Used as the baseline reference class for disease discrimination in the classification model.
Healthy
🍂
Yellow Leaf
Yellowing of leaves (chlorosis) may indicate nutrient deficiencies, overwatering, fungal infection, or natural senescence. Distinguishing this from other diseases is critical for correct treatment decisions.
Pathological

MoringaTriFusionNet_AU
A novel deep learning architecture that fuses three powerful CNN backbones using attention-based feature integration. Each branch extracts complementary feature representations, which are then adaptively fused to form a robust, generalized leaf disease classifier.

Trustworthy AI

Built for Reliability & Trust

Our system goes beyond raw accuracy — embedding transparency, uncertainty awareness, and human oversight at every prediction step.

🔍
Explainable AI
Using Integrated Gradients, the system generates pixel-level attribution maps that visually highlight which parts of the leaf influenced the model's classification decision. This transforms the "black box" into an interpretable diagnostic tool.
Integrated Gradients
📊
Uncertainty Estimation
MC-Dropout (Monte Carlo Dropout) performs multiple stochastic forward passes during inference to estimate prediction variance. A high uncertainty score signals that the model is not confident, prompting a human expert review before any action is taken.
MC-Dropout
🛡️
Reject Option — Expert Review
A Reject Option Classifier triggers an expert review flag whenever confidence is below threshold or uncertainty is too high. Instead of blindly outputting a wrong label, the system safely escalates ambiguous cases to domain experts — ensuring safe deployment in critical agricultural settings.
Reject Option Classifier
